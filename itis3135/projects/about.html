About Karen

Karen T. Jones is a professional photographer based in North Carolina, specializing in portrait, lifestyle, and event photography. With over a decade of experience, Karen blends technical skill with an artist's eye to capture timeless and heartfelt moments.

Her work is inspired by the natural beauty of people and places, and she is known for her warm, easygoing approach that puts clients at ease. Whether working with families, couples, or professionals, Karen delivers photographs that tell a story and celebrate life's milestones.
