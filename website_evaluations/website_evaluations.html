Website Evaluations

Coca~Cola

The home page of Coca~Cola.

  • Contrast: The contrast of this page is great. Everything on this page contrasts well. When text is in front of images it is easy to read, and understand.
  • Repetition: Each page linked on this site has a similar feel, and color scheme in some regard. All pages look good.
  • Alignment: Everything is in some order and looks well where it is on the page.
  • Proximity: Elements are not fighting for space on the page. There are diffrent sections for different topics.

Yale Art

This is an art page for Yale University.

  • Contrast: Seome sections on this page have decent contrast while other sections have terrible contrast.
  • Repetition: The page has repetition in the sense that each page it links to looks bad. Some elements are in similar spaces, but most colors and background are changed/different.
  • Alignment: This page is best at alignment, though it is not great at even that.
  • Proximity: The proximity of this page is terrible. Elements are all over the place and sections have no clear beginning or end.
